PM Grave Services was founded by Mr. Pitso Maleka in 2003. It is a Soweto based business that specializes in the closing, relocating,locating, and maintenance of graves and memorials for people who are unable to do this themselves. Grave sites of relatives are the memory of generations. Unfortunately, in most cases, in cemeteries there is no one to support this memory.
PM Grave Service's purpose is to provide quality and friendly grave services that ensures that the departed are buried with dignity and their final resting place is always presentable and treated with respect. We strive to exceed the expectations of the families and funeral service providers we serve and look to build lifelong relationships based on honesty, integrity, loyalty, and mutual respect.
The preferred grave services provider company with the objective of empowering and caring for the community.
Enhance the Customer Experience
In everything we do, we strive to meet and exceed the expectations of the families and funeral service providers we serve. We will be known in our community for our commitment to excellence in service, personnel and products.
